Projects

Separating GPT2 Chatbots from Humans Using Natural Language Processing

How realistic are the chatbots trained on Open AI's GPT2 compared to human casual conversation? In this project I try to separate the real from the simulated using Natural Language Processing and classification models.

A Better Sort of Yelp Photos

Why are the top photos in a Yelp gallery often blurry or dark? In this project I used photography 101 principles to train a convolutional neural net to pick the best photos in the gallery. This could provide automatic AI-powered sorting for restaurant owners.

Predicting Energy Demand

Myself and three collegues predict next hour energy demand using time series data. Such forcasts can help empower customers to lower their energy bill and/or maximize the proportion of their power that comes from renewable energy.
